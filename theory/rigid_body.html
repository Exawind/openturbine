R3xSO(3)-based Rigid Body

Overview

Details for a rigid-body solver. Unlike Bruls et al (2012), this is formulated in inertial coordinates.

Rigid-Body Model Input at Initialization

  • M* ∈ ℝ6×6: Rigid body mass matrix in material coordinate system (CS)

  • x0 ∈ ℝ3: Rigid body reference position; inertial CS

  • R0 ∈ ℝ3×3: Rigid body reference orientation

  • ui ∈ ℝ3: Rigid body initial displacement

  • Ri ∈ ℝ3×3: Rigid body initial orientation

  • u̇i ∈ ℝ3: Rigid body initial velocity

  • ωi ∈ ℝ3: Rigid body initial angular velocity

Degrees of Freedom

  • u ∈ ℝ3: Rigid body displacement at time t

  • R ∈ ℝ3×3: Rigid-body orientation at time t (represented as q̂ ∈ ℝ4 quaternion)

  • ω ∈ ℝ3: Rigid body angular velocity at time t

  • Generalized coordinates:

\[\begin{split}q = \begin{bmatrix} u \\ R \\ \end{bmatrix} , \quad \dot{q} = \begin{bmatrix} \dot{u} \\ \omega \\ \end{bmatrix} , \quad \ddot{q} = \begin{bmatrix} \ddot{u} \\ \dot{\omega} \\ \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

Residual

\[\begin{split}R^{\mathrm{rb}} = \begin{bmatrix} M^{\mathrm{rb}}\, \ddot{q} + g^{\mathrm{rb}} - F^{\mathrm{rb}} + B^T\lambda \\ \Phi \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{R}^{6+nc}\end{split}\]

where

\[\begin{split}B \in \mathbb{R}^{nc \times 6}\,, \quad \lambda \in \mathbb{R}^{nc}\,, \quad \Phi \in \mathbb{R}^{nc}\,, \quad F^{\mathrm{rb}} = \begin{bmatrix} f\\ m\\ \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{R}^{6}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}M^{\mathrm{rb}} = \begin{bmatrix} m I_3 & m \tilde{\eta}^T \\ m \tilde{\eta} & \rho \end{bmatrix} = \mathcal{RR}^0\, M^* {\mathcal{RR}^0}^T % \in \mathbb{R}^{6\times 6} \,,\quad g^{\mathrm{rb}} = \begin{bmatrix} m \tilde{\omega} \tilde{\omega} \eta \\ \tilde{\omega} \rho \omega \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]

and where

\[\begin{split}\mathcal{RR}^0 = \begin{bmatrix} R^0 & 0 \\ 0 & R^0 \end{bmatrix} \quad \text{for } \mathcal{RR}^0 \in \mathbb{R}^{6\times6}\end{split}\]

Time Integration

Iteration matrix:

\[\begin{split}S_t = \begin{bmatrix} M_t \beta'+C_t \gamma' + K_t T(h \Delta q) & B^T \\ B\,T(h \Delta q) & 0 \end{bmatrix} , \quad S_{t} \in \mathbb{R}^{(6+nc) \times (6+nc)}\end{split}\]

where

\[M_t = M^{\mathrm{rb}}\in \mathbb{R}^{6\times 6} \,, \quad C_t = \mathcal{G}^{\mathrm{rb}}\in \mathbb{R}^{6\times 6} \,, \quad K_t = \mathcal{K}^{\mathrm{rb}} \in \mathbb{R}^{6\times 6}\]

and where

\[\begin{split}\mathcal{G}^{\mathrm{rb}} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & \widetilde{ \widetilde{\omega} m \eta }^T + \widetilde{\omega} m \widetilde{\eta}^T\\ 0 & \widetilde{\omega} \rho - \widetilde{\rho \omega} \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\mathcal{K}^{\mathrm{rb}} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & \left( \dot{\widetilde{\omega}} + \tilde{\omega}\tilde{\omega} \right) m \widetilde{\eta}^T\\ 0 & \ddot{\widetilde{u}} m \widetilde{\eta} + \left(\rho\dot{\widetilde{\omega}} -\widetilde{\rho \dot{\omega}} \right) + \widetilde{\omega} \left( \rho \widetilde{\omega} - \widetilde{ \rho\omega} \right) \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]
